Ikot Abasi Tech Day 2022 (v2.0)
  • Ikot Abasi Local Government Council Hall
  • Saturday 15 January 2022

We are proud to present Ikot Abasi Tech Day 2022, a 1-day event aimed at driving awareness of the immense opportunity of the digital skill economy to indigenes and residents of the Ikot Abasi LGA and neighboring towns, cities, villages, etc. This conference will be a creative, interactive, and social networking experience to inspire you to collaborate in new ways.

Call for Speakers

Our Vision

To give our people the power to create, collaborate and share ideas to change the world without barriers

Our Goal

● Educate ● Collaborate ● Empower ● Innovate ● Employ ● Build

Our Mission

To add value, provide resources for technological advancement and raise public awareness of the benefits and opportunities of a digital and technology-skilled economy

Our Objectives

● Provide resources and guidance on how and where to get started in tech.

● Opportunity for participants to share diverse experiences in a conducive environment with like-minded individuals.

● Create opportunities for participants to collaborate.

● Solicit commitment from individuals and private and public institutions to invest in the people of Ikot Abasi

Encourage investment in infrastructure, donation/provision of facilities and equipment to facilitate the growth of the ecosystem in Ikot Abasi.

● Connect the people of Ikot Abasi to the neighboring tech communities in Eket, Uyo, Oron, Ikot Ekpene, and Port Harcourt.

● Showcase the economic, academic, and tourism potential of Ikot Abasi and Akwa Ibom State in general.

Why should you be there?

● Access to world-class information on the three pillars of ecosystem development: education, entrepreneurship, and employment.

● Opportunity for deserving and immersive members to gain access to support, mentorship, and possibly scholarship

● There are opportunities for you to network, share, and collaborate with peers from around Ikot Abasi and beyond.

● It will provide you with a transformative professional development experience

You will head home with new skills, fresh knowledge, and the motivation to apply what you’ve learned.

We look forward to meeting you at the Ikot Abasi Tech Day 2022. It would be a pleasure to network with you!
